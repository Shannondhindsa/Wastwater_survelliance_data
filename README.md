## Wastewater Surveillance Forecasting Dashboard

An interactive R Shiny dashboard for exploring SARS-CoV-2 wastewater surveillance data from the CDC's National Wastewater Surveillance System (NWSS) and forecasting short-term trends using ARIMA models.


## Why wastewater surveillance?

Wastewater testing offers a unique window into community-level pathogen transmission. Unlike case reporting, it doesn't depend on people seeking care or testing positive — it captures virus shed by everyone in a sewershed, including asymptomatic and untested individuals. Since COVID-19, NWSS has become one of the most important early-warning systems in U.S. public health.
